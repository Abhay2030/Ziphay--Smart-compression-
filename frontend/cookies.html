Cookie
Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026. We use cookies minimally and respectfully.

🍪 Short version: Ziphay uses zero advertising or tracking cookies. The only storage we use is your browser's localStorage to remember your theme preference (dark/light mode).

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. They're used to remember preferences and understand how people use websites. Ziphay uses a minimal approach.

Cookies Ziphay Uses

Essential
data-theme
Stored in: localStorage (not a server cookie) · Duration: Persistent until cleared · Purpose: Remembers your dark or light mode preference so the correct theme loads on your next visit.

Cookies We Do NOT Use

  • ❌ Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • ❌ Third-party tracking cookies (Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics, etc.)
  • ❌ Session tracking cookies for personalised ads
  • ❌ Cross-site tracking of any kind

Third-Party CDN Resources

Ziphay loads resources from Google Fonts and jsDelivr. These CDNs may set their own cookies or log access requests in accordance with their own policies. We recommend reviewing:

  • Google Fonts Privacy Policy
  • jsDelivr Privacy Policy

How to Control Cookies

You can clear localStorage and cookies in your browser settings at any time. Doing so will reset your theme preference to the default dark mode on your next visit.
