🔒 Short version: Ziphay processes your files entirely in your browser. We do not upload, store, or share your files. Ever. Read on for the full legal details.

1. Who We Are

Ziphay ("we", "us", "our") is a file compression and upscaling platform developed by Abhay Donde, based in Nashik, Maharashtra, India. For the purposes of this policy, "Ziphay" refers to the website and application available at ziphay.dev and related sub-pages.

2. The Core Privacy Principle

Ziphay's core compression and upscaling engine operates 100% client-side — entirely within your web browser. Your files are processed using the browser's Canvas API and JavaScript GZIP engine. No file data is ever transmitted to any Ziphay server.

This is not a marketing claim — it is an architectural fact. There is no server-side file processing pipeline. Your files never leave your device.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Files You Upload

Files you select or drag into Ziphay are processed locally in your browser using JavaScript. They are not uploaded to any server. They are not transmitted, logged, or stored by Ziphay.

3.2 Usage Data (Anonymised)

We may collect anonymised, non-identifying usage analytics (e.g., page views, compression feature usage counts) to improve the product. This data does not identify individual users and does not include file contents or file names.

3.3 Contact Form Data

If you contact us via the Contact page, the information you provide (name, email, message) is used solely to respond to your enquiry. It is not shared with third parties.

3.4 Cookies

Ziphay uses minimal cookies for theme preference persistence (dark/light mode). No tracking or advertising cookies are used. See our Cookie Policy for details.

4. How We Use Your Information

Any information collected is used exclusively to: (a) provide and improve the Ziphay service; (b) respond to contact enquiries; (c) detect and fix technical issues.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for commercial purposes. We do not use advertising networks. Ziphay products are ad-free.

6. Third-Party Services

Ziphay loads the following third-party resources: (a) Google Fonts — for typography (Syne, Outfit); (b) jsDelivr CDN — for the fflate compression library. These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them if you have concerns about CDN-level data collection.

7. Data Retention

Since Ziphay does not store your files, there is no file retention policy. Contact form submissions may be retained for up to 90 days for the purpose of responding to queries.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold (limited to contact form data). To exercise these rights, contact us at the Contact page.

9. Children's Privacy

Ziphay is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be noted in the Changelog. Continued use of Ziphay after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, contact Abhay Donde via the Contact page.
